Weather-aware outfit recommendations

Climate Closet helps you translate real-time weather into practical outfit ideas. Use your current location or any coordinates, see the forecast snapshot, and get clothing suggestions tuned to the temperature and your context (school, work, gym, date night, and more).

What you can do

  • Use browser geolocation or manual coordinates to fetch weather data.
  • See temperature, humidity, and wind in a compact dashboard.
  • Get simple outfit suggestions based on current conditions.
  • Connect to a Supabase wardrobe database and save favorite looks.
